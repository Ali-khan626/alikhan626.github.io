Plant-Description

Nooriabad Power Project comprises 2 x 50 MW Power Plants, which have been set up by two separate companies i.e. Sindh Nooriabad Power Company (Pvt) Ltd (SNPC) and Sindh Nooriabad Power Company Phase II (Pvt) Ltd (SNPC-II). This was done for speedy processing and early completion. Gas allocation of a total of 20 MMCFD was approved for the two plants by ECC. Government of Sindh earmarked 50 acres of land at Nooriabad, just outside the SITE domain. Each power plant consists of five 20V34SG Gas Engines, five Alborg Boilers and One Shin Nippon Steam Turbine. Switchyard equipment has been supplied by CHINT, while ABB has supplied Protection and Data Acquisition Systems. Each plant generates little more than 50 MW of power, and the plants, after meeting the auxiliary load, supply 100 MW to the Power Purchaser.
